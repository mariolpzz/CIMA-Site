ABOUT US 🧠

CIMA is the brainchild of three young student software developers who joined forces while working on a project for their studies. Recognizing the immense potential within their collaboration, they set out to breathe life into the games they had always dreamed of creating.

Our mission is to craft exceptional playable experiences, shattering the notion that one must be a major game studio with countless titles to produce something extraordinary. Instead, we believe that sheer determination, boundless passion, and unwavering dedication are the key ingredients for success. At CIMA, we pour our hearts into every aspect of game development, striving to delight players and push the boundaries of innovation.

Together, we aim to make our mark in the gaming industry, proving that a small group of devoted individuals can create gaming gems that resonate with audiences worldwide. Join us on this thrilling journey as we continue to challenge conventions and redefine what independent game development can achieve.

Blinded

"Blinded" is an online multiplayer horror game that aims to provide players with a good laugh with their friends while they battle in a tense environment to escape from an abandoned mansion. How did you end up there? You have no idea! You'll have to investigate the clues left by the former inhabitants to answer such questions and, ultimately, find a way to escape!
